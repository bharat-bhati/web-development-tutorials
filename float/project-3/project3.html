Travis Scott

Jacques Bermon Webster II, better known by his stage name Travis Scott, is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. His stage name is the namesake of a favorite uncle combined with the first name of one of his inspirations, Kid Cudi .He has been nominated for eight Grammy Awards and won a Billboard Music Award and a Latin Grammy Award.
